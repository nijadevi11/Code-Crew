Admissions

Your Path to BMSCE

Admissions are merit-based via KCET, COMEDK, JEE Main, GATE, and other national/state entrance tests.

Application Process

01
Appear for Entrance Exam
Register and appear for KCET (Karnataka residents) or COMEDK UGET. JEE Main scores also accepted for GoI quota seats.
02
Counselling & Seat Allotment
Participate in centralized KEA counselling (KCET) or COMEDK counselling based on your rank. Choose your preferred branch.
03
Document Verification
Report to BMSCE with original documents including 10th, 12th marksheets, entrance scorecard, and category certificate (if applicable).
04
Fee Payment & Enrollment
Pay the semester fee and complete enrollment formalities to confirm your seat. Hostel allocation done separately.
05
Orientation & Classes Begin
Attend the mandatory induction program, collect ID card, and begin your engineering journey at BMSCE.

About & Research

Legacy, Rankings & Innovation

Founded in 1946 by Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah, BMSCE was India's first private engineering college. Run by the BMS Educational Trust and located opposite the iconic Bull Temple in Basavanagudi, it has shaped over 40,000 engineers who lead organizations across the globe.
