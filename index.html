About & Research

Legacy, Rankings & Innovation

Founded in 1946 by Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah, BMSCE was India's first private engineering college. Run by the BMS Educational Trust and located opposite the iconic Bull Temple in Basavanagudi, it has shaped over 40,000 engineers who lead organizations across the globe.
